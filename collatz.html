Collatz conjecture calculator

The Collatz conjecture, also known as the 3n + 1 conjecture, is a mathematical sequence defined by the following rule: Start with any positive integer n. If n is even, divide it by 2; if n is odd, multiply it by 3 and add 1. Repeat the process, and the conjecture posits that no matter the starting value, the sequence will eventually reach the number 1.
